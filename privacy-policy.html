Cannon Bird

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2026

Overview

Cannon Bird is a mobile arcade game. This Privacy Policy explains what data the app stores, what data may be shared with third-party services, and how that data is used.

By using Cannon Bird, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores some gameplay data locally on your device, including:

  • High score
  • Total hits
  • Audio mute setting
  • Language setting
  • Generated player ID
  • Player name
  • Game over counter
  • Whether ads have been removed on the device

This data is stored locally so the game can remember your progress and settings.

Leaderboard Data

If the online leaderboard is enabled, Cannon Bird sends leaderboard-related data to the backend service used by the app.

This may include:

  • Player ID
  • Player name
  • High score
  • Total hits
  • Update timestamp

This data is used only to display and maintain the global leaderboard.

The current backend provider used by the app is:

  • Supabase

Advertising

Cannon Bird may display ads. Ads are currently served through:

  • Google AdMob

AdMob may collect device and usage information as described in Google's own privacy documentation. This can include advertising identifiers, device information, diagnostics, and interaction data used to deliver and improve ads.

If you purchase the ad-free option, interstitial ads are disabled for your device.

In-App Purchases

Cannon Bird may offer a one-time purchase to remove ads.

Purchases are processed by the platform store provider:

  • Google Play Store on Android
  • Apple App Store on iOS

The app itself does not collect or store your payment card details.

Sharing

The app includes a share feature that allows you to share a message about the game using other apps installed on your device. Cannon Bird does not separately store the content you choose to share.

Analytics

Cannon Bird does not currently use a dedicated analytics SDK beyond services needed for ads, purchases, and leaderboard operation.

If analytics are added later, this Privacy Policy should be updated before release of that version.

Children

Cannon Bird is a general audience game. If you plan to target children specifically, you should review Google Play and Apple App Store child-directed app requirements before publishing.

Data Retention

Local gameplay data stays on your device until you remove the app data or uninstall the app.

Leaderboard entries may remain on the backend until removed or replaced according to the app's leaderboard logic.

Your Choices

You can:

  • Change your player name in the app
  • Mute audio in the app
  • Purchase the ad-free option if offered
  • Stop using the app at any time
  • Remove local data by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage

Third-Party Services

The app may rely on these third-party services:

  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Billing / Apple In-App Purchase systems
  • Supabase

Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

Security

Reasonable steps are taken to use trusted platform and backend services, but no method of storage or transmission is guaranteed to be 100% secure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version should be made available with the app's store listing or official support page.
